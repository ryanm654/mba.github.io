Who We Are

Built for Competitors. Open to Everyone.

Manchester Battle Arena started as a handful of players gathering at a local venues like Fraggers, Arcade Club and Pixel Bar. Today, we're one of the North's most active FGC communities — running regular locals and community tournaments across all major titles.

Whether you're picking up a stick for the first time or grinding ranked, there's a place for you here. We believe the fighting game community grows stronger when everyone — from day-ones to veterans — plays together.

Community First

We're a welcoming space for players of all skill levels. No gatekeeping — just good games and good people.

Competitive Spirit

Regular tournaments and bracket play push everyone to level up. Casual and serious players both have a home here.

All Titles Welcome

Street Fighter, Tekken, Guilty Gear, Mortal Kombat — we run setups for all major titles at our sessions.
